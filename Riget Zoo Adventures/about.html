About Us



We here at Riget Zoo ensure that all customers are able to visit exotic animals in a setting that is confortable for both the visitor and the animals. We feel it is vital that we preserve as much wildlife as we can so our kids and their kids are able to be in the precense of such majestic creatures. We feel that one of the most important service that we provide is the knowlage that we can bestow upon both the youths and adults that visit us as we feel that the only way to truely appreciate our peers on earth we need to see them and learn about them and the struggles that they face due to our involvment in their lifestyle. We also have commited to treating our customers with a great experience and help them along the way as best as we can. One way that we do this is by offering the hotel as a place to rest in. This is important as due to the size and vastness of the animals that are available it is suggested to commit to a 2 day or three day trip in order to fully experience the attractions. In the hotel we offer our customers with a kind heart and a willing hand that will be trying to help you in any way possible.
