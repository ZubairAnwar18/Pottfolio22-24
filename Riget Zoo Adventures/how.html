Popular Animals

Ellie the Elephant

Ellie is one of our biggest animals but she also has the biggest heart. Ellie is gentle and extreamly playful she loves to use her long trunk to feel and show affection.

Charlie the Cheetah

Charlie is a young animal who can be grumpy and temperamental. However when he is happy he is just like a large housecat who loves to be played with and loves to be petted by the ones he trusts.

Lennie the Lemur

Lennie the lemur is a shy but curiouse animal. He loves puzzles and working to get his food or treats. He also likes to do tricks for visiting customers in order to get affection and treats.

Po the Panda

Po is a panda that loves to sleep. You might rarely catch him limbering over to on of the bamboo sticks that he loves to chow down on. But he is as lovely as he looks. Although it does take a lot of work to keep him clean and happy as he largely doesn't know what is best for him.

There are many, MANY more!
